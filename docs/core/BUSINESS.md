## üí∞ Revenue Model

### Fee Structure

**Platform Fee:** 0.5% AUM (50 basis points) annually

**Example:**
```
Customer AUM: $10M
Annual Revenue: $10M √ó 0.005 = $50k
Monthly Revenue: $4,166
```

### Revenue Sharing

**Client Revenue Share:** Configurable (default: 70% to client, 30% to platform)

**Example Calculation:**
```
End-user deposits: $1,000 USDC
Platform fee (50 bps): $5 to Quirk
Deposited to DeFi: $995
APY: 5% annually

After 1 year:
Yield earned: $49.75
Revenue split (70/30):
  - Client: $34.83
  - Quirk: $14.92
End-user receives: $34.82

Total end-user balance: $1,034.82
Effective APY for user: 3.48%
Client revenue: $34.83
Quirk revenue: $19.92 ($5 platform + $14.92 yield share)
```

### Customer Breakeven Analysis

**Minimum Viable AUM:**
```
Integration Cost: $5k-10k (one-time)
API Costs: $500-1k/month ($6k-12k/year)

Breakeven Calculation:
Need to cover $12k/year ongoing costs
$12k / 0.005 (50 bps) = $2.4M minimum AUM

Recommendation: Target customers with $10M+ AUM for comfortable margin
```

**Sweet Spot Customer ($10M AUM):**
```
Revenue: $50k/year
Cost: $10k integration + $12k/year = $22k first year
Net Benefit Year 1: $28k profit ‚úÖ
Net Benefit Year 2+: $38k/year profit ‚úÖ
Payback Period: 3-4 months
```

## üìú Regulatory Compliance (Singapore)

### Required Licenses

**1. MPIL (Major Payment Institution License)**
- **Covers:** Custody + On/Off ramp + Transfer
- **Cost:** ~$300-400K
- **Reference:** [MAS MPIL License](https://www.mas.gov.sg/regulation/payments/major-payment-institution-licence)

**2. CMS (Capital Markets Services License)**
- **Covers:** Fund Management
- **Cost:** ~$250K
- **Reference:** [MAS CMS License](https://www.mas.gov.sg/regulation/capital-markets/cms-licence)

**Total Licensing Cost:** ~$550-650K

**Strategy:** Start with partner solutions (TransFi, ZeroHash), get licenses later when scaled
